About Bear Art and Its Creator

Prior to marriage, Bryce Gardner made little to no effort to record his drawings. He developed his drawing abilities beginning at a young age during school, church, or travel. Unfortunately, Bryce's drawing abilities never developed beyond that of a five-year old. Losing interest in art, his skills then lay dormant for several years. They were reawakened in adulthood by drawing dinosaurs-tearing-humans-apart-type pictures to pass to girls in school. If they laughed, he knew he had a shot. They rarely laughed. Now Bear Art is a site to present and store the artwork of Bryce Gardner.
